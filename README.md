# Hiring Pipeline

AI-powered hiring tool for lean HR teams. Screen resumes, rank candidates with reasoning, send interview invites from your Gmail, and book interviews on Google Calendar — all from one dashboard.

---

## How It Works

**1. Sign in** with Google — one click, no setup.

**2. Create a role** — give it a title, done.

**3. Screen resumes** — upload PDFs directly or paste a Google Sheet ID linked to your application form. The AI ranks every candidate as Strong Fit, Moderate Fit, or Not Fit with a one-line reasoning for each.

**4. Send emails** — choose who to invite for interviews and who to reject from a clean and sorted dashboard. Emails go from your own Gmail account.

**5. Schedule interviews** — add your team's available time slots, click Match & Book. Calendar invites are sent to candidates and interviewers automatically.
